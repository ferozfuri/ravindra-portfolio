Fleet
Management

Overview

This Project is a web application which provides the platform for Fleet management to user who have travel Agency’s or any trasportation business. To maintain their fleet systamatically get the overview of their business.
Many transport agency’s still manage their business manually. Fleet Management make their work easy. It make their work easy, fast and scalable for large business. It has 7 different modules which makes Things fast and easy to access.

User Persona

Rajesh 30Yrs old from Hyderabad.
Travel Agency Owner Maintain his business alone. All records and stock etc were maintained manually.

Story

Rajesh is an owner of well reputed travel agency. He has 100 Fleets and he maintain them all alone. All the records, inventory, profits and losses were recorderd manually. Because of manual recording there were many mistakes in and the process was very slow and not accurate. Rajesh wants an application which make his work easy and fast.

Goals

* Record the data fater and accurately
* Predict the profit and losses.

Needs

* Build an web Application which records the data and also predict the losses and profits.

User Persona

Akshay 28Yrs Businessman from Pune
Transportation Business owner
He has trasportation business in different cities.

Story

Akshay is an owner of transportation business, which is in different cities. He has more than 200 Fleets under him and more than 20 Hub’s in different ciies and states, that makes his business complex till now akshay was tracking them manually by book records and man power. Akshay wants to maintian and trak their fleet online.

Goals

* Track the fleet, maintain record online.
* Schedule the next fleet, notification of damages and losses.

Needs

* Build an Web Application which can track all the fleet and hub’s, by their route, timing etc.

Challenge

As currently the users are maintaining their records manually in books & many of the user who works in the transport business are not that much educated. It was a bit challenging to make this complex into simple and minimalistic design so that any user can understand easily. -The main Challenge is to design an mobile application for the driver because many of the drivers are not educated or matric passed outs. And another challenge was to build the different modules for every thing for recorind the different data like fleet data, inventory, hub’s location wise data, driver information, vendors information and maintanence of fleet.

Solution

The solution is to design 7 different modules for different task which are interlinked with each other. The data which were recorderd manually buy the user in excel sheets can be uploaded directly into the application by a excel template.
